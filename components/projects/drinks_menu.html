India Rosa Drinks Menu

Overview

"A creative menu redesign for India Rosa, blending traditional Indian aesthetics with modern elegance. This project focused on typography, layout design, and visual storytelling to encapsulate the cultural richness and sophistication of the brand."

Mood Board

The mood board was inspired by:

  1. Color Palette: Deep brown, gold, red, and teal green to convey warmth, luxury, and vibrancy.
  2. Cultural Motifs: Indian elements like lotus flowers, henna patterns, and elephants for a distinct cultural identity.
  3. Typography: Elegant serif and modern sans-serif pairings to balance tradition with readability.

Key Features of the Design

  1. Typography:

    • Developed a clear hierarchy with bold serif fonts for titles (e.g., "India Rosa Drinks Menu") and clean sans-serif fonts for details.
    • Custom typographic style for “An achievement of the Sandhu family,” ensuring visual alignment with the brand identity.

  2. Illustration and Layout:

    • Integrated custom illustrations of traditional Indian women in vibrant attire, paired with cocktail visuals to link heritage with the restaurant's offerings.
    • Used intricate mandala designs as subtle background elements for an elegant touch.

  3. Menu Structure:

    • Organized sections for readability while maintaining visual richness.
    • Highlighted drinks with creative names, ingredient descriptions, and tiered pricing for a luxurious feel.

Challenges Met

  • Balancing intricate patterns and vibrant illustrations without overwhelming the layout.
  • Ensuring typography and design align with both Indian cultural elements and the modern, upscale branding of India Rosa.

Outcome

"Successfully redesigned the menu to reflect India Rosa's brand identity, creating a sophisticated dining experience that resonates with its target audience while celebrating cultural heritage."
