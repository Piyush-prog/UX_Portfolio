Conscious Cravings

Overview

With growing interest in sustainable living, this project aimed to design a food delivery app that promotes sustainability through features like eco-friendly filters and gamified rewards. The app targeted environmentally conscious users and sought to make sustainable decisions both convenient and rewarding. I led the entire UX process, including research, prototyping, and usability testing.

Research and Planning

Research for the project involved:

  1. Competitive Analysis:

    • Studied popular platforms like Uber Eats and DoorDash to identify gaps in sustainability features.
    • None of the existing platforms provided filters for eco-friendly packaging or offered incentives for sustainable behaviors.

  2. SCAMPER Ideation:

    • Brainstormed features to enhance usability and sustainability, including:

      • Rewards Systems: Points for choosing eco-friendly restaurants or zero-waste options.
      • Progress Tracking: Dashboards visualizing user contributions to sustainability.

Design and Implementation

  1. Wireframes:

    • Designed low-fidelity wireframes showcasing features like eco-friendly filters, a progress tracker, and a rewards dashboard.

  2. Usability Testing:

    • Conducted two rounds of testing to refine navigation and CTAs.
    • Users requested more intuitive icons and clearer labeling for eco-friendly options.

Findings

  • Gamified Rewards: Users were highly motivated by gamified rewards and progress tracking.
  • Navigation Clarity: Users wanted clearer visual cues for sustainable options and simplified navigation.

Challenges and Solutions

  • Challenge: Users were confused by certain icons and labels during testing.

    Solution: Updated icons with more dynamic and context-specific visuals.

  • Challenge: Limited engagement with rewards features in the first prototype.

    Solution: Redesigned the rewards dashboard to emphasize user progress and upcoming milestones.

Conclusion

This app demonstrated how gamification and sustainability can be integrated into an intuitive, user-friendly design. The final prototype balanced functionality and engagement, encouraging users to adopt eco-friendly habits without compromising convenience. This project solidified my expertise in iterative design and my passion for sustainability-focused UX.
