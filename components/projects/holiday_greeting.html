Holiday Greeting

Overview

Designed a festive collection of holiday wish cards inspired by the cozy theme of 'Gingerbread in Hot Chocolate.' The cards feature warm, playful illustrations and typography that evoke a sense of comfort, joy, and holiday cheer.

Process

Key Deliverables

  1. Theme Integration:

    • Incorporated gingerbread characters, marshmallows, and steaming mugs of hot chocolate as central visual elements.
    • Used textures and patterns reminiscent of cozy winter nights, like candy canes and whipped cream swirls.

  2. Typography:

    • Selected warm, hand-drawn style fonts to align with the gingerbread theme.
    • Paired playful headlines like "HO HO HO!" with elegant script fonts for a festive touch.

  3. Color Palette:

    • Featured rich browns, creamy whites, and festive reds and greens to reflect the gingerbread and hot chocolate theme.

  4. Illustrations:

    • Created custom illustrations of gingerbread cookies lounging in mugs, topped with whipped cream and sprinkles.

Outcome

Delivered a heartwarming set of wish cards that perfectly captured the whimsical and cozy feel of the holiday season, leaving a sweet impression with the unique 'Gingerbread in Hot Chocolate' theme.
