CCA

Overview

"Designed an elegant and informative leaflet for the Canadian Centre for Architecture’s (CCA) exhibition on Islamic Architecture. The design captures the intricate beauty of Islamic art and architecture, using a harmonious blend of traditional motifs, modern layouts, and cultural storytelling to engage and educate the audience."

Mood Board

The mood board was inspired by:

  1. Color Palette:

    • Purple for serenity and sophistication.
    • Gold for luxury and cultural depth.
    • Beige and white for balance and elegance.

  2. Cultural Motifs:

    • Geometric patterns, muqarnas, and mosaic tiles for visual intrigue.
    • Calligraphy-inspired typography to echo historical richness.
    • Iconic elements like domes, arches, and minarets for authenticity.

  3. Textures and Materials:

    • Marble and wood textures to represent the materials used in Islamic architecture.
    • Subtle gradients and overlays to add a modern touch.

Design Approach

  1. Typography:

    • Serif fonts for headers to evoke tradition and elegance.
    • Sans-serif fonts for body text to ensure readability and modernity.

  2. Visual Hierarchy:

    • Large, captivating images of Islamic landmarks to draw attention.
    • Clear sectioning for historical details and exhibition information.

  3. Layout and Composition:

    • Symmetrical grids to reflect balance, a key principle in Islamic art.
    • Decorative borders inspired by architectural patterns for a cohesive look.

Key Deliverables

  1. Final Leaflet Design:

    • Front and back designs showcasing exhibition details, architectural insights, and the CCA’s branding.
    • Integration of motifs and patterns as background elements for a layered aesthetic.

  2. Mood Board:

    • A curated visual guide to highlight inspirations and design elements.

Challenges and Solutions

  • Challenge: Balancing intricate patterns with readability.

    Solution: Used a clean layout with ample white space to prevent visual overload.

  • Challenge: Capturing the essence of Islamic architecture without oversaturating the design.

    Solution: Incorporated subtle patterns and a limited color palette to maintain sophistication.

Outcome

"The leaflet successfully conveyed the richness of Islamic architecture while aligning with the modern and educational brand identity of the CCA. It enhanced the audience's experience by merging design with cultural storytelling."
